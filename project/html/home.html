Description

MemeMory is a special online version of the popular game MemoryÂ®. The peculiarity of MemeMory is that all the images depict very recent memes from reddit. You can either play against a computer, which comes in four different difficulty levels, or you can compete online against other players.

INFO: Currently, multiplayer is limited to 1 vs. 1.

Instructions

The game decides "randomly" who may start. When it's your turn, you get to open two cards in a row. If those two cards are the same, they remain face up, you get one point and it's your turn again. If the cards are different, they are turned over again and it is the next player's turn.
